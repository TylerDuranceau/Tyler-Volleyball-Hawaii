My Volleyball Journey

I grew up in Hawaii, where volleyball is a big part of the culture. Playing the sport has taught me discipline, teamwork, and perseverance. It has also given me some of my closest friendships.

Life in Hawaii

Hawaii’s beaches and volleyball courts shaped a lot of my life. The balance between school, practice, and games has helped me grow not just as an athlete but as a person.

Quick Facts

  • I play as an outside hitter or opposite hitter.
  • One of my goals is to help my college win our conference.
  • Volleyball has given me lifelong friends, both in Hawaii and in college.

Hawaii Inspiration

Hawaii is where my love for volleyball began — it’s a place full of energy and community.
